Life OS

Your personal command center

Private • Multi-Module • Mobile-Friendly

Life OS is a single-user hub that mirrors a modern productivity suite: projects, tasks, calendar, goals/habits, health, finances, notes, reminders, and wishlist—each tailored to your tracking profile so you only see what you care about.

Core Experience
  • Personalized onboarding & “Tailor Profile” controls.
  • Dashboard cards that respect your module toggles.
  • Quick add + edit directly on Tasks, Events, Reminders, Notes.
  • Mobile-friendly layout with dark theme.
Modules
  • Projects & Tasks: Kanban, priorities, quick add/edit.
  • Calendar: events/appointments with status tracking.
  • Goals & Habits: streaks, targets, reflections.
  • Finance & Health: budgets/accounts and workout logs.
  • Notes, Reminders, Wishlist: fast capture and management.
Security & Access
Sign-in sessions honor “Remember this browser”; all module pages require authentication; write endpoints use lightweight token gating when enabled.
